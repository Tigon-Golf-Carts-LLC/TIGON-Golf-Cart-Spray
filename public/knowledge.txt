# APPLICATION KNOWLEDGE
# =====================

## Best Practices
1. Always apply to cool surfaces in shade
2. Use clean microfiber cloths
3. Work in sections for best coverage
4. Allow product to dry before use
5. Store in cool, dry place

## Common Questions
Q: How often should I clean my golf cart?
A: Weekly cleaning is recommended for regular use

Q: Can I use on electric golf carts?
A: Yes, avoid electrical components and battery terminals

Q: Is rinsing required?
A: No, all products are no-rinse formulas

Q: How long does protection last?
A: Original: 7 days, Pro Shield: 30 days, Elite: 45 days
